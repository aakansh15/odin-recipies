Sandwich Recipe

Description

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type. The sandwich began as a portable, convenient finger food in the Western world, though over time it has become prevalent worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Bread: 2 slices (white, wheat, multigrain, or your preference)
  • Butter: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Mayonnaise: 2 tablespoons (use vegan mayo if desired)
  • Vegetables: Choose your favorites! Here are some options:
    • Cucumber: 2-3 slices
    • Tomato: 1-2 slices
    • Lettuce: 2-3 leaves
    • Bell pepper: 1/4 cup, thinly sliced
    • Onion: 1/4 cup, thinly sliced (optional)
    • Sprouts: 1/4 cup (optional)
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the bread: If desired, toast the bread lightly in a toaster or pan with butter.
  2. Spread the mayo: Spread one tablespoon of mayonnaise on each slice of bread.
  3. Layer the vegetables: Arrange your chosen vegetables evenly on one slice of bread.
  4. Season: Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Assemble the sandwich: Top with the other slice of bread, mayo side down.
  6. Cut and serve: Cut the sandwich in half (diagonally or straight) and enjoy!
